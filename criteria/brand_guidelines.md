# Olive Street Band — Brand Guidelines

> These are the hard design rules for all band art.  
> Nothing ships without meeting every criterion in this file.

---

## 1. Core Identity

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Band name | **Olive Street Band** |
| Genre | Funk and R&B cover band |
| Tagline | **None** — do not add taglines |
| Text in logos | `OLIVE STREET BAND` only — no abbreviations, no "OSB" |

---

## 2. Shape

- **All logos must be circular / badge-style**
- Circular format is the primary output shape for profile images
- All elements must stay within the 88% safe-zone radius of the canvas

---

## 3. Colors

See `criteria/color_palette.json` for exact hex + RGB values.

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary | Olive Green | `#6B7A3A` |
| Secondary | Tan | `#DDB892` |
| Accent | Cream | `#F5F0E1` |

- **Max 3 colors** in any single design
- Do not introduce new colors without explicit approval
- Dark scheme: olive green background, tan + cream for text/elements
- Light scheme: cream background, olive green text

---

## 4. Typography

See `criteria/typography.md` for the full type system.

- Text: `OLIVE STREET BAND` — uppercase, full name, no abbreviations
- Letter-spacing: `font_size * 0.20–0.30` for the band name
- No taglines, no secondary copy (no "FUNK SOUL RHYTHM", no genre labels)

---

## 5. Logo Don'ts

- No olive imagery — no literal olives, olive branches, or olive fruit
- No "OSB" monogram or other abbreviations
- No taglines or extra text beyond the band name
- No gradients on the band name text itself
- No drop shadows on the primary logotype
- No more than 3 colors

---

## 6. Size and Quality

| Property | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Canvas | 1536×1536 px (current standard; started at 1024×1024) |
| Circular safe zone | All elements within 88% of the canvas radius |
| Small-size test | Must read clearly at 64×64 px equivalent |
| Output format | SVG (master), PNG optional via cairosvg |

---

## 7. Design Direction (What's Working)

After 16 designs across 6 rounds, the direction has shifted **away from** literal symbols
(olives, music notes, instruments as the primary mark) and **toward** graphic/geometric
treatments used as the central motif:

- Waveforms and soundbars
- Oscilloscope traces
- Vinyl record geometry (rings, grooves)
- Typographic badges and stamps
- Abstract radial / concentric patterns

Keep exploring in this geometric/graphic direction.

---

## 8. Approval Checklist

Before any design is considered final:

- [ ] Circular format
- [ ] Text reads `OLIVE STREET BAND` — no more, no less
- [ ] Max 3 colors, all from the approved palette
- [ ] No olive imagery
- [ ] No taglines
- [ ] Reads clearly at 64×64 px
- [ ] All elements within 88% of canvas radius
- [ ] SVG source file saved in `output/logos/`
